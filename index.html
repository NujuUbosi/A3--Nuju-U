MY CONNECTION TO AFROBEATS

I feel at home listening to Afrobeats. I grew up with this sound; it's more than music; it's my culture, my identity, and the beat of my native Nigeria. The pulsating streets of Lagos, my home, and the immense pride I have as a member of a culture that has an impact on the entire globe are brought to life with each beat. No matter where I am, Afrobeats always put me in touch with my roots and make me feel grounded and energized. Everything about his music,the rhythms, the songs, the tales—transports me to a place of comfort and familiarity, where I can feel the love and acceptance of my family and the comforts of home. As a Nigerian girl with boundless energy, imagination, and a heritage that follows me around like a flag, it inspires me, makes me feel better, and keeps me grounded in my identity.

Favourite Afrobeat's Artist

ASAKE

Asake's music breathes life into every song by fusing Yoruba culture with urban sounds and strong choir-like layering. He has a vibrant, soulful, and uniquely Nigerian sound. His music seems like a joy of our origins because of the way he blends traditional elements with contemporary Afrobeats.
